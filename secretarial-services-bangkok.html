Corporate Secretarial Services Bangkok for SMEs and Foreign Companies


Secretarial Services Bangkok helps keep your company in good shape. There are always small things to update like records, forms, and regular filings. Most people don’t have time or interest for that. So we take care of it. If there is any change in directors or shareholders, we fix the paperwork. If something needs to be submitted, we send it and follow up. When BOI or licenses come in, we guide you slowly so you understand what’s going on. You run your business. We look after the formal work in the background. Simple and clean.
