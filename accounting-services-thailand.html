Accounting

Accounting Services Thailand

At Baan Banchi.com, we help companies across Thailand manage their accounting properly. Some of our clients are small local firms just starting out. Others are international companies with busy offices here. Big or small, they all want the same thing: clean books and reliable numbers.

We handle bookkeeping, reports, tax filing, and compliance. Once we take over, you can focus on your business. You’ll always know how things look financially without any guess work.

Why It Matters

Good accounting keeps a business healthy. When your books are up to date, you can see where the money goes, what’s working, and what needs attention. It also makes tax time calm instead of chaotic.

We meet many clients every month to go through their cash flow and reports. Most of them say those short meetings help them understand their business better. If you want to look up the official rules yourself, the Revenue Department of Thailand has all the information.

What We Do

Our Accounting services in Thailand cover everything from daily bookkeeping to year-end financial statements.

Set up of Accounting Systems
We help set up accounting systems that suit each business. Some prefer cloud software. Others still like to keep things on-site. Either way, we make sure everything matches Thai accounting standards.
Accounting under various GAAP
Our accountants prepare reports under both Thai GAAP and IFRS. That way, your statements make sense for management here and for head offices abroad.
Expense Processing, Voucher Preparations, and Cheque Writing Support
We record expenses, prepare vouchers, and handle cheque work. It may sound routine, but it saves hours later and keeps your records tidy.
Maintenance of Various Books & Records
We maintain all your financial books so you can check your company’s position anytime. Nothing is left half done or outdated.
Reporting to Parent Companies
If your business reports to a parent company, we prepare the statements in the exact format they need. It keeps communication smooth and avoids last-minute corrections.
Preparing Year-End Financial Statements
At the end of each year, we prepare financial statements that meet Thai accounting and tax standards. They’re ready for audit and filing right on time.
Analytics
We don’t just record data. We review it with you, highlight trends, and point out what can improve. You’ll see where profits rise and where costs can drop.

Why Businesses Choose Baan Banchi.com

Our bilingual team speaks Thai and English, so there’s no mix-up in communication.Clients stay with us because we’re easy to reach, careful with details, and deliver when promised. We also handle audits, HR, and compliance so everything connects under one roof.
